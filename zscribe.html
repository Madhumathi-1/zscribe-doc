The word "scribe" can have a few different meanings, depending on the context:

Noun:

A scribe is a person who writes or copies documents by hand, often in earlier times before the printing press was invented.

Verb:

To scribe is to write or copy something by hand.

Noun:

In some ancient cultures, a scribe was a person who not only wrote but also was responsible for record-keeping, often within the government or religious institutions.

Noun:

In carpentry and woodworking, a scribe refers to a tool used to mark or cut a line on a surface in order to fit it to another surface.
